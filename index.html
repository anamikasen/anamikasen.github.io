About Me

Hey there, I am Anamika and I have been working as a Software Engineer at Microstrategy Inc. since Aug 2019.

During my tenure here, I have worked on some very interesting projects ranging from application development of an internal load testing tool which was presented in MicroStrategy World 2020 to working on cloud orchestration where we deploy the software on Microsoft Azure.

My tech stack currently involves a lot of Java, Python, Bash and Powershell, Azure and AWS. I have also worked on development of RESTful APIs. I have delivered independent features as well as worked on resolving high priority production issues.

I enjoy challenges and love working in a collaborative environment. Always eager to learn and grow more!
